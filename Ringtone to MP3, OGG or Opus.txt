Tested on Android Oreo only. To use this flowchart, you need FFmpegui (app id: "org.ffmpeg.gui"). It can be downloaded for free from the Google Play Store:

https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.ffmpeg.gui

This flow automates FFmpegui to convert the "melody.wav" file to MP3, OGG or Opus. The wave file (unsigned 8000Hz PCM) is generated by the following flowchart (ringtone composer):

https://llamalab.com/automate/community/flows/42796
