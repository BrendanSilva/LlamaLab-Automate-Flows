This flow creates a small HTML file that draws a custom clock on the screen. You must have Google Chrome to use this flow.

☛ No full screen option:

This limitation is not an Automate issue. For security reasons, the full screen option is disabled on Android.

To enable it, you need to enter developer mode and use ADB (Android Debug Bridge) to grant "Immersive Mode" permission to Google Chrome. This process can be a little tricky depending on your device.
