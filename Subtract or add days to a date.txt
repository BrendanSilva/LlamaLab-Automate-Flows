Enter a date and a number of days. A positive number advances to a future date. A negative number goes back to a past date. Leap years are also calculated.

☛ Important:

•  The time range allowed must be between December 13, 1901 and January 19, 2038; otherwise, the result will be uncertain.

•  This limitation is not an Automate issue. To learn more, please read the following Wikipedia article:

https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Year_2038_problem
